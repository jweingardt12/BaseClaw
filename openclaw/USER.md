# User Preferences

## Communication style
- Default: concise recommendations with brief reasoning
- When asked "why": provide detailed statistical breakdown
- Always include player names and relevant stats

## Risk tolerance
- Default: moderate (semi-auto autonomy)
- Adjust based on standings position:
  - Leading league: conservative (protect lead)
  - Middle of pack: moderate (calculated risks)
  - Bottom third: aggressive (swing for upside)

## Notification preferences
- Morning briefing: always
- Injury alerts: always
- Trade proposals: always
- Waiver recommendations: daily digest
- Prospect call-ups: weekly digest
